Location

Lagos — Where You Belong

Rua de Pallos de La Frontera, São Gonçalo de Lagos — a quiet, well-connected neighbourhood just minutes from the historic heart of Lagos. You're a short walk from the marina, the old town walls, and some of the Algarve's most loved beaches.

Lagos is the beating heart of the western Algarve expat scene. Thousands of British, Dutch, German, and Scandinavian families have made it home — drawn by the climate, the culture, the safety, and a quality of life that is genuinely hard to find anywhere else in Europe.

The Clube de Ténis e Padel de Lagos is just steps away — a great asset for sports enthusiasts and families alike.
